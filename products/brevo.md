# Brevo

**Category:** Marketing & Newsletters
**Type:** SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU (France)
**Website:** [brevo.com](https://www.brevo.com/)

## What It Is
Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) is an all-in-one platform for email marketing, SMS, marketing automation, and transactional emails.

## Hosting Options
SaaS only. Data is stored and processed exclusively on servers within the EU.

## Compliance Notes
- Headquartered in Paris, France.
- Fully GDPR compliant with built-in tools for consent management and data deletion.
- No US CLOUD Act exposure as it is an EU-owned legal entity.
