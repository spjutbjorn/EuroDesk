# Matomo

**Category:** Web Analytics
**Type:** Self-hosted / SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU compliant (InnoCraft)
**Website:** [matomo.org](https://matomo.org/)

## What It Is

Matomo (formerly Piwik) is a powerful, open-source Google Analytics alternative that protects your data and your customers' privacy.

## Hosting Options

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Self-hosted** | Run Matomo on your own EU infrastructure |
| **Matomo Cloud** | Use a managed service if it meets your sovereignty requirements |

## Why it fits EuroDesk

- **Data Ownership:** You own 100% of your data when self-hosted on your own EU infrastructure.
- **GDPR Compliance:** Features built-in tools to easily anonymize data, respect Do Not Track, and comply with EU privacy laws without compromising on insights.
- **Capabilities:** Full-featured analytics including heatmaps, session recordings, and A/B testing, rivaling enterprise US tools.

## Compliance Notes

- **Jurisdiction:** Matomo is developed by InnoCraft, based in New Zealand (EU Adequacy Decision). It is widely regarded as the most GDPR-compliant analytics tool in Europe.
- **Data Ownership:** You own 100% of your data. When self-hosted on your own EU infrastructure, no data is shared with third parties.
- **Privacy:** Supports cookieless analytics and automatic anonymization of IP addresses. Recommended by many EU privacy regulators (e.g., CNIL).
- **GDPR:** Fully compliant as a self-hosted or managed EU-hosted tool. No cross-site tracking across different Matomo instances.
