# Mixpost

**Category:** Social Media Management
**Type:** Self-hosted / SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU (Czech Republic)
**Website:** [mixpost.app](https://mixpost.app/)

## What It Is
Mixpost is an open-source social media management application. It allows marketing teams to plan, schedule, and publish content across multiple social networks (Twitter/X, Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, Mastodon, etc.) from one unified dashboard.

## Hosting Options
Can be fully self-hosted on your own EU infrastructure. They also offer an EU-hosted Cloud version.

## Plans (2026)

| Plan | Price (One-time) | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Lite** | $0 | Self-hosted, basic features |
| **Pro** | ~$299 | Unlimited accounts, 1yr updates |
| **Enterprise** | ~$1,199 | White-labeling, SaaS capabilities |

> **Note:** Mixpost uses a "pay-once" model for updates, making it a highly cost-effective sovereign alternative to Buffer or Hootsuite.

## Key Features
- Multi-platform scheduling and publishing.
- Shared content calendar.
- Analytics and reporting dashboards.
- Team collaboration and approvals.

## Compliance Notes
- Developed by Inovector, based in the Czech Republic (EU).
- Essential for PR and Communications teams handling embargoed or sensitive corporate announcements, ensuring drafts are not stored on US-based platforms like Hootsuite or Buffer.
- Fully open-source and GDPR compliant.
