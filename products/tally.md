# Tally

**Category:** Form Builder
**Type:** Managed SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** Belgium (EU)
**Website:** [tally.so](https://tally.so/)

## What It Is

Tally is a lightweight form builder with a document-like editing experience. It is well suited for low-friction internal or external forms when a team wants a simple EU-based managed option.

> **Note:** Tally is highly privacy-conscious and stores data on EU servers (Frankfurt) by default.

## Why it fits EuroDesk


- **Jurisdiction:** Belgian company with EU-focused operations.
- **Usability:** Very fast to deploy for everyday business forms.
- **Privacy:** Better jurisdictional fit than many US form tools.

## Compliance Notes

- Avoid collecting more personal data than necessary in public forms.
- Review where file uploads are stored and who can access submissions.
- For highly sensitive workflows, prefer self-hosted forms such as LimeSurvey instead.
