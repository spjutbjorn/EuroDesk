# Excalidraw

**Category:** Whiteboarding & Collaboration
**Type:** Self-hosted (Exception) / SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** US/Global (Open Source / Self-Hosted Exception)
**Website:** [excalidraw.com](https://excalidraw.com/)

## What It Is
Excalidraw is a virtual, collaborative whiteboard tool that lets you easily sketch diagrams that have a hand-drawn feel.

## Hosting Options
Can be fully self-hosted via Docker on EU infrastructure. There is also an enterprise product (Excalidraw+).

## Compliance Notes
- **Self-Hosting Exception:** Applies because it is open-source.
- Supports end-to-end encryption.
- An excellent sovereign alternative to Miro or Mural for Product Managers, Consultants, and Designers.
