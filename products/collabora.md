# Collabora Online

**Category:** Office Suite
**Type:** Self-hosted or managed
**Jurisdiction:** Germany / United Kingdom (adequacy-approved)
**Website:** [collaboraonline.com](https://www.collaboraonline.com/)

## What It Is

Collabora Online is a LibreOffice-based office suite for collaborative editing in the browser. It integrates well with Nextcloud and similar platforms and provides strong compatibility with Microsoft Office and OpenDocument formats.

## Hosting Options

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Self-hosted** | Run Collabora Online on your own EU-owned infrastructure |
| **Managed by EU provider** | Use a deployment operated by an EU or adequacy-country provider |

## Plans (Support 2026)

| Plan | Price (User/Year) | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| **CODE (Dev Edition)** | Free | For home use and testing; no SLA |
| **Business** | ~$23.18 | Up to 99 users, LTS, SLA, security updates |
| **Enterprise** | ~$18.82 | 100+ users, roadmap input, integration help |

> **Collabora Online Development Edition (CODE)** is the free version for home users and small technical teams who do not require a professional SLA or signed security updates.

## Why it fits EuroDesk


- **Data sovereignty:** Documents can stay on infrastructure you control.
- **Privacy:** Works well with EU-hosted storage platforms like Nextcloud.
- **Format support:** Strong support for DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, and OpenDocument files.

## Compliance Notes

- Prefer self-hosting or an EU-operated managed deployment to avoid foreign cloud exposure.
- Keep document storage in an EU-hosted file platform rather than inside non-EU collaboration suites.
- Disable any external analytics in the surrounding reverse proxy or hosting stack.
