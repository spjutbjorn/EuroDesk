# Cal.com

**Category:** Scheduling & Booking
**Type:** Self-hosted (Exception) / SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** US (Open Source / Self-Hosted Exception)
**Website:** [cal.com](https://cal.com/)

## What It Is
Cal.com is an open-source alternative to Calendly for meeting scheduling and booking.

## Hosting Options
Must be self-hosted on EU-owned infrastructure to satisfy EuroDesk requirements, as the SaaS version may be subject to US jurisdiction.

## Compliance Notes
- **Self-Hosting Exception:** Applies because it is open-source and can be self-hosted.
- Ensure any telemetry or external analytics are disabled in the self-hosted environment to maintain strict GDPR compliance.
