# BigBlueButton

**Category:** Webinars & E-Learning
**Type:** Self-hosted
**Jurisdiction:** Global (Open Source / Self-Hosted Exception)
**Website:** [bigbluebutton.org](https://bigbluebutton.org/)

## What It Is
BigBlueButton is a highly robust open-source web conferencing system designed primarily for online learning, training, and large-scale webinars.

## Hosting Options
Fully self-hostable. Extensively hosted and offered as a managed service by major EU providers (e.g., Hetzner, Infomaniak) to ensure full GDPR compliance for schools and corporate training.

## Compliance Notes
- **Self-Hosting Exception:** Open-source software.
- The standard choice for EU educational institutions and public administration looking to avoid Zoom or Microsoft Teams for large meetings.
- Can be tightly integrated into Nextcloud and Moodle.
