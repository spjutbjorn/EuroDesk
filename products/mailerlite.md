# MailerLite

**Category:** Marketing & Newsletters
**Type:** SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU (Lithuania / Ireland)
**Website:** [mailerlite.com](https://www.mailerlite.com/)

## What It Is
MailerLite is a user-friendly email marketing platform offering newsletters, automation, and landing pages.

## Hosting Options
SaaS only. Hosted on EU servers.

## Plans (Marketing 2026)

| Plan | Price (Month) | Key Limits |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | 500 subscribers, 12k emails/mo |
| **Growing Bus.** | Starts $10 | Unlimited emails, 3 users |
| **Advanced** | Starts $20 | AI assistant, custom HTML editor |

## Key Features
- Drag-and-drop newsletter builder.
- Automation workflows and RSS-to-email.
- Landing pages and signup forms.
- Paid newsletter subscriptions.

## Compliance Notes
- EU company (headquartered in Ireland, origins in Lithuania).
- Full GDPR compliance.
- No US CLOUD Act exposure.
