# Pimcore

**Category:** Digital Asset Management (DAM) & Master Data
**Type:** Self-hosted / PaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU (Austria)
**Website:** [pimcore.com](https://pimcore.com/)

## What It Is
Pimcore is a leading open-source enterprise platform for Digital Asset Management (DAM), Master Data Management (MDM), and Product Information Management (PIM). It centralizes all marketing content, product data, and rich media in one single hub.

## Hosting Options
The core platform is open-source and free to self-host on EU infrastructure. Pimcore also provides an enterprise Cloud Edition.

## Compliance Notes
- Headquartered in Salzburg, Austria.
- Open-source and fully sovereign, crucial for managing a company's entire digital catalog (images, videos, metadata) without relying on US platforms like Bynder or Cloudinary.
- Deep focus on B2B data complexities and full compliance with GDPR when handling structured corporate data.
