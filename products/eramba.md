# Eramba

**Category:** Governance, Risk & Compliance (GRC)
**Type:** Self-hosted
**Jurisdiction:** Global (Open Source / Self-Hosted Exception)
**Website:** [eramba.org](https://www.eramba.org/)

## What It Is
Eramba is a leading open-source GRC (Governance, Risk, and Compliance) platform. It helps organizations track compliance (GDPR, ISO 27001, SOC2), manage risk registers, and perform internal audits.

## Hosting Options
Must be self-hosted via Docker on EU-owned infrastructure to maintain full control over sensitive audit and compliance data.

## Key Features
- Risk management and risk register.
- Compliance management (ISO, GDPR, etc.).
- Internal and external audit tracking.
- Policy management and awareness.

## Compliance Notes
- **Self-Hosting Exception:** Applies because the Community Edition is open-source and self-hostable.
- Indispensable for Risk Managers, CISOs, and DPOs to map regulatory requirements against actual internal controls.
- As it stores highly sensitive data regarding an organization's vulnerabilities and compliance gaps, self-hosting on a sovereign EU server is paramount.
