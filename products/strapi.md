# Strapi

**Category:** Content Management System (Headless CMS)
**Type:** Self-hosted / SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU (France)
**Website:** [strapi.io](https://strapi.io/)

## What It Is
Strapi is the leading open-source Headless CMS. It is 100% JavaScript and fully customizable, allowing developers and content creators to build and deploy rich web APIs for modern corporate websites, mobile apps, or digital displays.

## Hosting Options
The core software is open-source and free to self-host on your own EU infrastructure. Strapi Cloud is available as a managed SaaS solution.

## Compliance Notes
- Developed by a French company headquartered in Paris.
- Open-source Node.js foundation.
- A strong sovereign alternative to proprietary headless CMS vendors like Contentful, providing maximum control over website data and publishing workflows.
