# Penpot

**Category:** Design & Prototyping
**Type:** Self-hosted or managed
**Jurisdiction:** Spain (EU)
**Website:** [penpot.app](https://penpot.app/)

## What It Is

Penpot is an open-source design and prototyping platform for design, product, and engineering teams. It is a strong European alternative to Figma and uses open web standards such as SVG.

## Hosting Options

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Self-hosted** | Run Penpot on your own EU infrastructure |
| **Penpot Cloud** | Use a managed deployment from an EU vendor |

## Why It Fits EuroDesk

- **Jurisdiction:** Built by a Spanish company.
- **Self-hosting:** Sensitive design assets can stay on your own infrastructure.
- **Open standards:** Reduces vendor lock-in on design files and workflows.

## Compliance Notes

- Prefer self-hosting for sensitive design assets, prototypes, and client work.
- Store uploaded assets and exports on EU-controlled infrastructure.
- Limit public sharing links and use SSO where possible.
