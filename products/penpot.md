# Penpot

**Category:** Design & Prototyping
**Type:** Self-hosted / SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU (Spain)
**Website:** [penpot.app](https://penpot.app/)

## What It Is

Penpot is an open-source design and prototyping platform meant for cross-domain teams. It is a true, viable alternative to Figma, entirely web-based, and uses SVG as its native format.

## Why it fits EuroDesk

- **Jurisdiction:** Created by Kaleidos Open Source, based in Madrid, Spain.
- **Data Sovereignty:** Can be fully self-hosted via Docker, ensuring design assets never leave your servers.
- **Standards:** Uses open web standards (SVG) meaning no vendor lock-in on your design files.

## Alternatives Considered

| Alternative | Why not |
|---|---|
| Figma | US company, proprietary format, no self-hosting |
| Sketch | US company, proprietary, macOS only |
