# Plausible Analytics

**Category:** Web Analytics
**Type:** Self-hosted or managed
**Jurisdiction:** Estonia (EU)
**Website:** [plausible.io](https://plausible.io/)

## What It Is

Plausible is a lightweight, privacy-first web analytics tool. It avoids cookies and cross-site tracking and is designed to be easier to operate under European privacy expectations than traditional analytics suites.

## Hosting Options

| Option | Description |
|---|---|
| **Plausible Cloud** | Managed service from an EU vendor |
| **Self-hosted** | Run the open-source stack on your own EU servers |

## Why it fits EuroDesk


- **Jurisdiction:** Built and operated from the EU.
- **Privacy-first:** No cookies, no cross-site tracking, and minimal data collection.
- **Flexible deployment:** Managed or self-hosted depending on your control needs.

## Compliance Notes

- Keep the deployment cookie-free and avoid enabling invasive cross-site tracking patterns.
- Host analytics data in the EU and restrict raw access to reporting roles.
- Document retention periods and honor deletion or export requests where applicable.
