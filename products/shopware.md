# Shopware

**Category:** E-commerce
**Type:** Self-hosted / SaaS
**Jurisdiction:** EU (Germany)
**Website:** [shopware.com](https://www.shopware.com/)

## What It Is
Shopware is a leading open-source e-commerce platform in Europe, known for its flexibility and API-first approach.

## Hosting Options
Can be self-hosted on EU infrastructure or used via their EU-hosted cloud offering.
