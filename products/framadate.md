# Framadate

**Category:** Scheduling & Booking
**Type:** Self-hosted / Managed
**Jurisdiction:** EU (France)
**Website:** [framadate.org](https://framadate.org/)

## What It Is
Framadate is an open-source online service for planning an appointment or making a decision quickly and easily, offered by the French non-profit Framasoft.

## Hosting Options
Can be used via the Framasoft-hosted instance in France, or fully self-hosted on your own EU infrastructure.

## Compliance Notes
- Fully subject to GDPR.
- Zero tracking and no data exploitation.
- Developed by a privacy-focused non-profit.
