# PrestaShop

**Category:** E-commerce
**Type:** Self-hosted / Managed
**Jurisdiction:** EU (France)
**Website:** [prestashop.com](https://www.prestashop.com/)

## What It Is
PrestaShop is a feature-rich, open-source e-commerce solution used by hundreds of thousands of online stores.

## Hosting Options
Best self-hosted on EU-owned infrastructure (e.g., Hetzner, OVHcloud) to maintain full data sovereignty.

## Compliance Notes
- Open-source software developed in France.
- Allows complete control over customer data.
- Ensure the chosen hosting provider is an EU legal entity to avoid US CLOUD Act exposure.
