# Paperless-ngx

**Category:** Document Management & Archiving
**Type:** Self-hosted
**Jurisdiction:** EU (Open Source)
**Website:** [docs.paperless-ngx.com](https://docs.paperless-ngx.com/)

## What It Is
Paperless-ngx is an open-source document management system that transforms your physical documents into a searchable online archive. It uses OCR to make scanned documents searchable and automatically extracts metadata.

## Hosting Options
Fully self-hosted via Docker. It should be hosted on EU infrastructure to maintain strict control over sensitive legal, real estate, and administrative documents.

## Compliance Notes
- Completely open-source and community-driven.
- No vendor lock-in. Data is stored locally on your server.
- Excellent for compliance workflows requiring secure document retention and fast retrieval (e.g., Legal, Real Estate, HR, Public Admin).
